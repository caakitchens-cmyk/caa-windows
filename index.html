Project Types

Different projects need different inputs — we keep it simple and practical.

New Build / Builder

Usually quoted from plans. Send your PDF drawings and window schedule.

  • Best for: builder packages, new homes, multi-stage builds
  • What we need: plans + elevations + window/door schedule
  • Optional: preferred system/colour, hardware requirements

Renovation / Replacement

Often requires onsite check to confirm openings, reveals, and install conditions.

  • Best for: replacing existing windows/doors
  • What we need: photos + rough sizes + address/suburb
  • Measure: scheduled if required after initial review
